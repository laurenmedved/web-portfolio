Structured Education:

Structured Education is an early learning website that I made during my coursework in Graphic Design 2 at Baldwin Wallace University.

Above is an embedded final presentation.

Below includes several personas that I developed while performing research on the intended audience. After creating a prototype for this website I also conducted several user-testing studies that involved meeting with families and asking them and their children to navigate the website. I took all of their feedback into consideration when developing the final product.

In addition to personas, I also have several screenshots of the website. This website was created mostly using Bootstrap framework with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
